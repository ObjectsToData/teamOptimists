ON THE TOWNS


Published: March 31, 1996

An opinionated guide to cultural and recreational goings-on around the state this week. To submit items for consideration, write to On the Towns, Sunday New Jersey Section, The New York Times, 229 West 43d Street, New York, N.Y. 10036, or send a fax to (212) 556-7219.

MUSIC

CATHEDRAL BASILICA OF THE SACRED HEART, NEWARK The Music Department presents "Good Friday Music," a program of choral music from the 18th and 19th centuries in a meditation on the Passion and death of Jesus. Friday at 8 P.M. Free. Clifton Avenue, Newark. (201) 484-4600.

CHATHAM HIGH SCHOOL AUDITORIUM The Colonial Symphony presents "Symphony for Strings," by Richard Nanes, "Three Pieces in Olden Style," by Henryk Gorecki and "Metamorphosen," by Richard Strauss. April 12 at 8:30 P.M. Tickets: $10-$32; $5 for students. 255 Lafayette Avenue, Chatham. (908) 766-7555.

COMMUNITY CONGREGATIONAL CHURCH, SHORT HILLS Choirs from New York City and New Jersey will join to sing Mozart's Requiem in a special Palm Sunday concert. Today at 4 P.M. Free. 200 Hartshorn Drive, Short Hills. (201) 379-5600.

COMMUNITY THEATER OF MORRISTOWN The Operas-in-Concert series continues with a semi-staged production of Gounod's "Faust," featuring Jianyi Zhang, a Metropolitan Opera tenor, and Oksana Krovytska, a New York City Opera soprano. Today at 3 P.M. Tickets: $25-$35. 100 South Street, Morristown. (201) 539-8008.

FAIRLEIGH DICKINSON UNIVERSITY "From Mozart to Messiaen" with the All Seasons Chamber Players. Today at 3 P.M. Free. Edward Williams College Auditorium, 150 Kottle Place, Hackensack. (201) 692-7031. "Russian Music: Uncovering Scores in the F.D.U. Library Collection," with Valeriya Tuz, vocalist and pianist, and Dr. James H. Fraser, director of the campus library. Tuesday at 8 P.M. Free. "A Roman Holiday: The Legend of Mario Lanza," with the Opera at Florham Guild. Saturday at 8 P.M. Free. Lenfell Hall, the Mansion. 285 Madison Avenue, Madison. (201) 443-8620.

HOUSE OF PRAYER EPISCOPAL CHURCH, NEWARK Chorus 802 presents Mozart's Requiem. Today at 3 P.M. Free; donations accepted. 407 Broad Street, Newark. (201) 483-8202.

MAXWELL'S The Spinanes. Tonight at 9. Tickets: $7. 16 Deluxe. Wednesday at 9 P.M. Tickets: $7. Jonathan Richman. Thursday at 9 P.M.; Friday and Saturday at 10 P.M. Tickets: $10. 1039 Washington Street, Hoboken. (201) 798-4064.

MONTGOMERY CULTURAL CENTER "Yesterday Is Today," featuring music of Bach. Today at 4 P.M. Tickets: $10; $5 for students and the elderly. 124 Montgomery Road, Skillman. (609) 921-3272.

NEW JERSEY SYMPHONY ORCHESTRA The Winter Pops Series continues. Today at 3 P.M. Symphony Hall, Newark. Tickets: $18-$37. (800) ALLEGRO or (201) 624-8203.

STATE THEATER Mike Burstyn performs Broadway favorites, Israeli popular songs and the Yiddish theater music. Today at 2 P.M. Tickets: $16-$25. 15 Livingston Avenue, New Brunswick. (908) 246-7469.

TENAFLY PUBLIC LIBRARY A concert by young musicians, directed by Dorothy Kaplan Roffman. April 13 at 5 P.M. Free. 401 Tenafly Road, Tenafly. (201) 568-8680.

TURNING POINT Megan McDonough and Debi Smith. Tonight at 7:30. Tickets: $15. John Whelan and Friends. Thursday at 8 P.M. Tickets: $15. Paul Geremia. Friday at 9 P.M. Tickets: $10. The Blues Jumpers. Saturday at 10 P.M. Tickets: $10. 468 Piermont Avenue, Piermont, N.Y. (914) 359-1089.

Theater

BICKFORD THEATER, MORRIS MUSEUM "Driving Miss Daisy." Today at 2 P.M. and Thursday at 8 P.M. Tickets: $17.50; $15 for students and the elderly. Morris Museum, 6 Normandy Heights Road, Morristown. (201) 538-8069.

COUNT BASIE THEATER Phoenix Productions presents "Jesus Christ Superstar." Friday at 8 P.M.; Saturday at 3 and 8 P.M.; April 14 at 3 P.M. Tickets: $15-$19; $2 discount for the elderly. 99 Monmouth Avenue, Red Bank. (908) 747-0014.

CROSSROADS THEATER COMPANY "Fear Itself," by Eugene Lee. Today and next Sunday at 3 and 7:30 P.M.: Tuesday, Wednesday and Friday at 8 P.M.; Thursday at 11 and 8 P.M.; Saturday at 3 and 8 P.M. Tickets: $22-$32. 7 Livingston Avenue, New Brunswick. (908) 249-5560.

ELIZABETH PLAYHOUSE "Bus Stop," by William Inge. Today and next Sunday at 2 P.M.; Friday and Saturday at 7:30 P.M. Tickets: $8; $6 for students and the elderly. 1100 East Jersey Street, Elizabeth. (908) 355-0077. FOUNDATION THEATER "Macbeth." Today at 2 P.M. Tickets: $15; $13 for the elderly and $5 for students. Burlington County College, Route 530, Pemberton. (609) 894-2138.

GLASSBORO CENTER FOR THE ARTS The American Repertory Ballet presents "Romeo and Juliet." Saturday at 8 P.M. Tickets: $13.50-$18.50. Rowan College, Glassboro. (609) 256-4545.

MANALAPAN HIGH SCHOOL THEATER "Evita." Today at 2 P.M. Tickets: $8; $4 for the elderly. Church Lane, Manalapan. (908) 431-8320.

McCARTER THEATER "The Misanthrope," a comedy by Moliere, starring Stephen Lang. Today at 2 P.M. Tickets: $24-$32. 91 University Place, Princeton. (609) 683-8000.

PAPER MILL PLAYHOUSE "Call Me Madam," by Irving Berlin, starring Leslie Uggams. Opens Wednesday. Through May 26. Wednesdays and Fridays at 8 P.M.; Thursdays at 2 and 8 P.M.; Saturdays and Sundays at 2 and 8 P.M. Tickets: $31-$46. Brookside Drive, Millburn. (201) 376-4343.

PARK PERFORMING ARTS CENTER "The Passion Play." Through April 21. Saturdays and Sundays at 2 P.M. Tickets: $20; $18 for the elderly. 560 32d Street, Union City. (201) 865-6980.
