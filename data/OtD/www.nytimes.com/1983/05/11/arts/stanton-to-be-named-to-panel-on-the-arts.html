Stanton to Be Named To Panel on the Arts

AP
Published: May 11, 1983

WASHINGTON, May 10— Frank Stanton, the president of CBS Inc. from 1946 to 1971, will be appointed to the President's Committee on the Arts and the Humanities, the White House announced today.

Mr. Stanton will succeed Nancy Hanks, who died Jan. 7.
