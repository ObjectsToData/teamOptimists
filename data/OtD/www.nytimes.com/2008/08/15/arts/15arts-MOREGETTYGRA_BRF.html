More Getty Grants for New Orleans

  • Sign In to E-Mail or Save This
  • Print
  • Reprints
Compiled by Julie Bloom
Published: August 14, 2008

The Getty Foundation has announced a third and final round of grants to support the recovery of visual arts organizations in New Orleans. Some 21 grants totaling $2.8 million have been awarded to museums and arts organizations so far. The latest total more than $1 million and are timed to coincide with the third anniversary of Hurricane Katrina. Recipients include the Cathedral of St. Louis, the Louisiana Endowment for the Humanities, the New Orleans Jazz and Heritage Foundation, the Contemporary Arts Center, the Louisiana State Museums, the New Orleans Cultural Coalition and Prospect.1 New Orleans. Prospect.1, a biennial to open in November, will feature the work of 81 local, national and international artists in over 100,000 square feet of exhibition space throughout the city.
