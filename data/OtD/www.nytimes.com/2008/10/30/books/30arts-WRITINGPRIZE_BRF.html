Writing Prize Winners Are Announced

  • Sign In to E-Mail or Save This
  • Print
  • Reprints
Compiled by DAVE ITZKOFF
Published: October 29, 2008

The Mrs. Giles Whiting Foundation announced the recipients of the 2008 Whiting Writers’ Awards, bestowed annually on poets, fiction and nonfiction writers, and playwrights “of exceptional talent and promise in early career.” This year’s awards, each of which comes with a cash prize of $50,000, go to the fiction writers Mischa Berlinski, Laleh Khadivi, Manuel Muñoz, Benjamin Percy and Lysley Tenorio; the essayist Donovan Hohn; the poets Rick Hilles, Douglas Kearney and Julie Sheehan; and the playwright Dael Orlandersmith. The foundation, which supports the humanities and writing, was established in 1963 by Flora E. Whiting, and its writing prize was first awarded in 1985.
