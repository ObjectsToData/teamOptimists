Elnaz Menhaji, David Klotz

  • Sign In to E-Mail or Save This
  • Print
  • Reprints
Published: June 15, 2008

Elnaz Menhaji, a daughter of Badrolsadat Menhaji and Mohsen Menhaji of Greenvale, N.Y., was married on Friday to David Matthew Klotz, a son of Nancy H. Klotz and Lt. Gen. Frank G. Klotz of Bolling Air Force Base in Washington. Leslie Gross, the town clerk of North Hempstead, N.Y., officiated in her office there. On Sunday evening, Ahmad Pakzad is to lead a traditional Persian ceremony at the Westbury Manor, a caterer in Westbury, N.Y.

Skip to next paragraph

The bride and bridegroom will begin postdoctoral fellowships in August, she in chemistry at Columbia and he in the humanities through the American Council of Learned Societies, which is also in New York. They met at Yale, from which they received doctorates last month, the bride in organic chemistry and the bridegroom in Egyptology.

Ms. Menhaji-Klotz, 27, graduated from Barnard. Her parents own Port Jewelers in Port Washington, N.Y.

Mr. Klotz, 26, graduated from Yale, from which he also has two master’s degrees, one in mathematics and the other in Egyptology. His father is the assistant vice chief of staff and the director of the Air Force staff at the Pentagon.
