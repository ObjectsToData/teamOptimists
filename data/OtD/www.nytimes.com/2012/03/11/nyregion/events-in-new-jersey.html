Comedy

Film

State Theater, 15 Livingston Avenue. statetheatrenj.org; (732) 246-7469.

PRINCETON JUNCTION West Windsor Arts Center Friday Film Series: “Born Into Brothels,” a documentary written and directed by Zana Briski and Ross Kauffman.
