A GUIDE TO SUMMER FESTIVALS OF DANCE


Published: May 31, 1987

The following is a selective list of summer dance festivals and events. It is national in scope but concentrates on the Northeast. New York City

AGLAIA DANCE PRODUCTIONS New and established young modern-dance choreographers and performers will be presented during the weekends in June at the Middle Collegiate Church: Toni Wisti (June 4-5); Susan Hamlin and Laura Roe (June 6-7); Nancy Safian and Mia Lawrence (June 11-12); Nancy Alfaro and Jane Setteducato (June 13-14); Susan Hefner Kline (June 20-21); Sam Yip (June 27-28). (Middle Collegiate Church, 50 E. Seventh St., New York, N. Y. 10003. Phone: 228-8108) ART IN THE ANCHORAGE Creative Time, an arts organization for visual and performing artists, will present its fourth annual monthlong installation/performances, discussions and benefit events in the cathedral-like interior of the Brooklyn Bridge Anchorage, located at Cadman Plaza West and Front Street in Brooklyn: Huck Snyder's installation/performance ''Circus'' (Thurs.-Sats., through June 27); John Torreano and Friends (June 10); Guerrilla Girls (June 16); John Kelly (June 22); Blondell Cummings and Gretchen Langheld and Musicians (June 30). (Creative Time, 66 W. Broadway, New York, N. Y. 10007. Phone: 571-2206) ART ON THE BEACH Another of Creative Time's summer series of exhibitions and collaborative performances has found a new home in outdoor space on the waterfront at Hunters Point, Queens, across from Manhattan: Mark Antrobus (June 28); Tamar Rogoff (July 8, 12); Stephen Laub (July 15, 19); Julia Hayward (July 25-26); Perry Hoberman (Aug. 12, 16); Tamara Jenkins (Aug. 19, 23); Yvonne Meier (Aug. 30, Sept. 2); Kei Takei (Sept. 13, 16).
