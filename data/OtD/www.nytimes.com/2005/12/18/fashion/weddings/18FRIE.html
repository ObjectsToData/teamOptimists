Asia Friedman and Jeremy Olshan

  • Sign In to E-Mail This
  • Printer-Friendly
  • Reprints
  • Save Article
Published: December 18, 2005

Asia Friedman and Jeremy Olshan were married last night at Battery Gardens, a restaurant in Manhattan. Callie Janoff, a minister of the Church of Craft, a nondenominational group, officiated, with Rabbi Marcia Rappaport taking part.

Skip to next paragraph

Forum: Weddings and Celebrations

The couple, both 31, graduated from Sarah Lawrence College, where they met.

Ms. Friedman is keeping her name. She is pursuing a Ph.D. in sociology at Rutgers University. She received a master's degree in humanities and social thought from New York University. She is the daughter of Jennifer Lawrence of San Francisco and William Friedman of Philadelphia, and the stepdaughter of Jennifer Friedman and of the late Peter Scholes.

Mr. Olshan is a transit reporter for The New York Post. He received a master's degree in journalism from Columbia. He is a son of Lois Olshan and Samuel Olshan of Forest Hills, Queens.
