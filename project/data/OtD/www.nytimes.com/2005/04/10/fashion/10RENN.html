ina Leslie Rennert, a daughter of Dr. Morton C. Rennert of Cliffside Park, N.J., and the late Susan Sider Rennert, was married last evening to Jonathan Edward Cohen, the son of Ruth and Robert Cohen of Silver Spring, Md. Rabbi Stacy Friedman officiated at the Faculty Club on the campus of the University of California, Berkeley.

The bride and bridegroom teach at the Prospect Sierra School in El Cerrito, Calif., where they met. They both received master's degrees in education from Stanford University.

The bride, 31, teaches humanities to the seventh grade. She graduated cum laude from American University and completed a Fulbright teaching assistantship in Déville-lès-Rouen, France

The bride's father, a periodontist, practices in New York.

The bridegroom, 36, teaches science in the sixth and seventh grades. He graduated from Oberlin College.

His mother, who retired as a clinical social worker in private practice in Silver Spring, Md., was also a president of the Association of Marriage and Family Therapy in Washington. His father retired as the general counsel and executive director of the National Association of Social Workers, a trade group in Washington.
