A guide to cultural and recreational goings-on in and around Connecticut. Items for the guide should be sent at least three weeks in advance to ctcal@nytimes.com, or by mail to Connecticut Calendar, Metropolitan, 620 Eighth Avenue, New York, N.Y. 10018-1405.

Comedy

FAIRFIELD Fairfield Theater Company Pauly Shore. May 20 at 7 and 10 p.m. $42 and $52. Fairfield Theater Company, 70 Sanford Street. (203) 259-1036; fairfieldtheatre.org.

MASHANTUCKET Comix at Foxwoods Nick Di Paolo. Thursday to May 14. $25 to $50. Comix at Foxwoods, 240 MGM Grand Drive. comixatfoxwoods.com; (866) 646-0609.

MASHANTUCKET MGM Grand, Foxwoods Casino Jerry Seinfeld. Friday and May 14. $82.50 to $150. MGM Grand, Foxwoods Casino, 240 MGM Grand Drive. mgmatfoxwoods.com; (866) 646-0050.

Film

HARTFORD Cinestudio “Jane Eyre,” directed by Cary Fukunaga. Wednesday through May 14. $7 and $8. “Potiche,” directed by François Ozon. May 15 through May 19. $7 and $8. Cinestudio, 300 Summit Street. cinestudio.org; (860) 297-2463.

HARTFORD Real Art Ways “Some Days Are Better Than Others,” directed by Matt McCormick. Through Thursday. “Uncle Boonmee Who Can Recall His Past Lives,” directed by Apichatpong Weerasethakul. Friday and May 14. “Certified Copy,” directed by Abbas Kiarostami. Friday through May 19. $4.50 to $9. Real Art Ways, 56 Arbor Street. realartways.org; (860) 232-1006.

RIDGEFIELD Ridgefield Playhouse “The Met Live in HD: Wagner’s Die Walküre,” simulcast of the opera. May 14 at noon and 6 p.m. $25. Ridgefield Playhouse, 80 East Ridge Road. (203) 438-5795;

ridgefieldplayhouse.com.

WASHINGTON Gunn Memorial Museum “Through the Tunnel,” documentary on the first year of racial integration at public schools in Manatee County, Fla. Thursday at 6:30 p.m. Free. Gunn Memorial Museum, 5 Wykeham Road. (860) 868-7756; gunnlibrary.org.

WATERBURY Palace Theater of Connecticut “The Connecticut Student Film Festival,” films by middle- and high-school students. Friday, 4 to 8 p.m. Free. Palace Theater of Connecticut, 100 East Main Street. (203) 755-4700; palacetheaterct.org.

For Children

ANSONIA Ansonia Nature Center “Look at the Birdie,” bird watching and craft activities. Ages 3 to 5. Thursday at 10:30 a.m. $5 and $7. Ansonia Nature Center, 10 Deerfield Road. ansonianaturecenter.org; (203) 736-1053.

BRIDGEPORT Downtown Cabaret Theater “Goldie Locks,” musical comedy. Ages 3 and up. Through May 22. $18. Downtown Cabaret Theater, 263 Golden Hill Street. downtowncabaret.org; (203) 576-1636.

FAIRFIELD Edgerton Center for the Performing Arts “Patty’s Green,” musical about the environment. Ages 3 to 8. May 21 at 2 p.m. $9 to $15. Edgerton Center for the Performing Arts, 5151 Park Avenue. edgertoncenter.org; (203) 371-7908.

FAIRFIELD Fairfield Theater Company “Sunday Afternoon of Magic,” comedy, magic and juggling. All ages. May 22 at 3 p.m. $12 to $22. Fairfield Theater Company, 70 Sanford Street. fairfieldtheatre.org; (203) 259-1036.

FAIRFIELD The Community Theater “The Westport Youth Film Festival,” screenings of film by area students, workshops and panel discussions. Ages 8 and up. May 14, 10 a.m. to 9 p.m. Passes are $7 to $35. Workshops are $20 and $25. The Community Theater, 1424 Post Road. (203) 222-7070 ext. 112; wyff.net.

OLD SAYBROOK The Katharine Hepburn Cultural Arts Center “The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe,” musical based on the story by C. S. Lewis, presented by TheatreWorks U. S. A. May 15 at 3 p.m. $10 and $16. The Katharine Hepburn Cultural Arts Center, 300 Main Street. (877) 503-1286; katharinehepburntheater.org.

RIDGEFIELD Ridgefield Playhouse “Dance Across China,” traditional Chinese dances featuring area students. Ages 4 and up. (203) 438-5795; ridgefieldplayhouse.com.

WESTPORT Long Lots Elementary School “101 Dalmatians Kids,” musical based on the Disney classic. Friday and May 14. $12 to $18. Long Lots Elementary School, 13 Hyde Lane. musictheatreofct.com; (203) 454-3883.
