ON THE TOWNS


Published: April 21, 1996

An opinionated guide to cultural and recreational goings-on around the state this week. To submit items for consideration, write to On the Towns, Sunday New Jersey Section, The New York Times, 229 West 43d Street, New York, N.Y. 10036, or send a fax to (212) 556-7219.

MUSIC

BERGEN COUNTY TECHNICAL SCHOOLS THEATER The State Repertory Opera presents Mozart's "Magic Flute." Saturday at 7:30 P.M. Tickets: $35; $30 for students and the elderly. 200 Hackensack Avenue, Hackensack. (201) 763-7969.

CRESCENT THEATER The Greater Trenton Symphony Orchestra, conducted by John Peter Holly, presents a pops concert featuring music by George Gershwin and Richard Rodgers. Today at 3 P.M. Tickets: $10-$25. 50 North Clinton Avenue, Trenton. (609) 394-1338.

DELBARTON SCHOOL, MORRISTOWN The Northern Morris Choral Society and Orchestra perform a benefit concert for the Interfaith Council for Homeless Families of Morris County. Next Sunday at 2:30 P.M. Tickets: $25-$50; $15 for students. 230 Mendham Road, Morristown. (201) 822-1528.

MAURICE LEVIN THEATER Weslia Whitfield, jazz cabaret singer. Tonight at 7. Tickets: $26. Jewish Community Center of Metropolitan New Jersey, 760 Northfield Avenue, West Orange. (201) 736-3200, extension 253.

MAXWELL'S The Little Kings with Scott Kempner and Mike Mesaros. Tuesday at 9 P.M. Tickets: $7. Nancy Boy. Thursday at 9 P.M. Tickets: $8. The Knockout Drops. Friday at 10 P.M. Tickets: $7. The Health and Happiness Show and Valentine Smith. Saturday at 10 P.M. Tickets: $7. 1039 Washington Street, Hoboken. (201) 798-4064. MONTCLAIR HIGH SCHOOL COMMUNITY AUDITORIUM Unity Concerts of New Jersey presents Awadagin Pratt, pianist. Today at 3:30 P.M. Tickets: $15-$30. Park and Chestnut Streets, Montclair. (201) 744-6770.

MONTCLAIR STATE UNIVERSITY The Preservation Hall Jazz Band of New Orleans. Saturday at 8 P.M. Tickets: $25; $20 for the elderly and $10 for students. Memorial Auditorium, Valley Road and Normal Avenue, Upper Montclair. (201) 655-5112.

NEW JERSEY SYMPHONY ORCHESTRA The Ovation Series, "Beethoven and Beyond," conducted by Mariusz Smolij. Today at 3 P.M., John Harms Center for the Arts, Englewood. Tickets: $12-$36. (800) ALLEGRO or (201) 624-8203.

PARSIPPANY UNITED METHODIST CHURCH Carmen Artis, soprano, and John Hammel, tenor, present "Cole on the Coals: A Celebration of Cole Porter Love Songs." Next Sunday at 3 P.M. Tickets: $12; $8 for students and the elderly. South Beverwyck Road, Parsippany. (201) 328-0082.

PASCACK HILLS HIGH SCHOOL Valentina Kozlova and Leonid Kozlov present "Swan Lake." Today at 2 P.M. Tickets: $15. Grand Avenue, Montvale. (201) 664-7090.

PEDDIE SCHOOL Gabriela Imreh, pianist, and the Philadelphia Virtuosi Chamber Orchestra, conducted by Daniel Spalding, present a benefit concert for the Richard L. Swig Center. Today at 2 P.M. Tickets: $15. South Main Street, Hightstown. (609) 490-7550.

RUTGERS UNIVERSITY The university Brass Choir. Saturday at 2 P.M. Free. Nicholas Music Center, George Street at Route 18, New Brunswick. (908) 932-7511.

TENAFLY PUBLIC LIBRARY The Friends of the Tenafly Public Library present the Ridgewood Gilbert and Sullivan Opera Company in "The Sorcerer." Next Sunday at 2:30 P.M. Free. 401 Tenafly Road, Tenafly. (201) 568-8680.

TIERNEY'S TAVERN The Sunday Nite Big Band Bash presents the James L. Dean Jazz Orchestra. Tonight at 6. Tickets: $6. 136 Valley Road, Montclair. (201) 744-9785.

TURNING POINT Tom Chapin. Today at 5 and 8 P.M. Tickets: $17.50. Laurie Lewis and Grant Street. Wednesday at 8 P.M. Tickets: $14. Preston Reed. Thursday at 8 P.M. Tickets: $10. Commander Cody and his Lost Planet Airmen. Friday at 7:30 and 10:30 P.M. Tickets: $17.50. Jimmy Vivino and the Black Italians. Saturday at 8 and 11:30 P.M. Tickets: $10. Livingston Taylor. Next Sunday at 5:30 and 8:30 P.M. Tickets: $17.50. 468 Piermont Avenue, Piermont, N.Y. (914) 359-1089.

UNION COUNTY ARTS CENTER The Westfield Symphony Orchestra, conducted by Brad Keimach. Saturday at 8 P.M. $23; $19 for the elderly; $12 for students. 1601 Irving Street, Rahway. (908) 499-8226.

WATCHUNG ARTS CENTER Bucky Pizzarelli and Gene Bertoncini, jazz guitarists. Thursday and Friday at 8 P.M. Tickets: $10. Pam Purvis, jazz vocalist. Saturday at 8 P.M. Tickets: $10. 18 Stirling Road, Watchung. (908) 753-0190.

ZIMMERLI ART MUSEUM The spring concert series begins with Natassia Xsavier, harpsichordist and Mary Schmidt, flutist. Today at 3 P.M. Free. Rutgers University, George and Hamilton Streets, New Brunswick. (908) 932-7237.

THEATER

ALL SAINTS PARISH, HOBOKEN The Will Atkins Theater Project presents "Voices From the Shelter II," a dramatic reading of works by residents and friends of the Hoboken Clergy Coalition Shelter for the Homeless. Saturday at 8 P.M. Tickets: $6. 700 Washington Street, Hoboken. (201) 792-3563.

BROOKDALE COMMUNITY COLLEGE "South Pacific," by Rodgers and Hammerstein. Today and next Sunday at 3 P.M.; Friday and Saturday at 8 P.M. Tickets: $16; $13 for students and the elderly. Performing Arts Center, 76 Newman Springs Road, Lincroft. (908) 224-2411.
