ON THE TOWNS


Published: March 24, 1996

An opinionated guide to cultural and recreational goings-on around the state this week. To submit items for consideration, write to On the Towns, Sunday New Jersey Section, The New York Times, 229 West 43d Street, New York, N.Y. 10036, or send a fax to (212) 556-7219.

MUSIC

CITY HALL COURT ROOM The Hoboken Acoustic Music Series continues with the Gripweeds and Ghost Rockets. Friday at 7:30 P.M. Tickets: $5. 94 Washington Street, Hoboken. (201) 420-2207.

CRANDON HIGH SCHOOL The New Jersey Intergenerational Church Orchestra presents "Sights, Sounds and Touch." Thursday at 7:30 P.M. Free. West End Place, Crandon. (908) 561-3802.

COMMUNITY THEATER OF MORRISTOWN The Operas-in-Concert series continues with a semi-staged production of Gounod's Faust, featuring Jianyi Zhang, a Metropolitan Opera tenor, and Oksana Krovytska, a City Opera soprano. Next Sunday at 3 P.M. Tickets: $25-$35. 100 South Street, Morristown. (201) 539-8008.

ERIC BROWN THEATER The Carnegie Chamber Players present a concert featuring the works of Mozart, Martinu and Brahms. Saturday at 8 P.M. Tickets: $15; $10 for students and the elderly. Jewish Community Center on the Palisades, 411 East Clinton Avenue, Tenafly. (201) 569-7900, extension 376.

FAIRLEIGH DICKINSON UNIVERSITY Recreation Center. "Traditional Music of China," featuring Yi Yang, zitherist, and Tao Chen, flutist. Today, 3 to 5 P.M. Tickets: $5; $2 for children and the elderly. Hartman Lounge, 285 Madison Avenue, Route 124, Madison. (201) 443-8661.

FIRST CONGREGATIONAL CHURCH, MONTCLAIR "Outpost in the Burbs" presents Cheryl Wheeler. Friday at 9 P.M. Tickets: $12. 40 South Fullerton Avenue, Montclair. (201) 744-6560.

JERSEY CITY STATE COLLEGE The Concert Chorale presents Gabriel Faure's "Requiem" Today at 4 P.M. Free. St. Aedan's Church, 800 Bergen Avenue, Jersey City.The college's voice faculty and students present a concert. Wednesday at noon. Free. Hepburn Hall, 2039 Kennedy Boulevard. (201) 200-3151.

MAXWELL'S Mysteries of Life. Wednesday at 9 P.M. Tickets: $6. The Gufs. Thursday at 9 P.M. Tickets: $5. Flat Duo Jets and Pscylone Rangers. Friday at 10 P.M. Tickets: $7. Disappear Fear and Daniel Cartier. Saturday at 10:30 P.M. Tickets: $8. 1039 Washington Street, Hoboken. (201) 798-4064.

MONTCLAIR HIGH SCHOOL COMMUNITY AUDITORIUM The Maestro Series of Unity Concerts continues with Theresa Santiago, soprano. Today at 3:30 P.M. Tickets: $15-$20. Park and Chestnut Streets, Montclair. (201) 744-6770.

NEW JERSEY SYMPHONY ORCHESTRA The Masterworks Series continues with Nokuthula Ngwenyama, violist, and Christopher Collins Lee, violinist, performing Beethoven, Debussy and Grieg. Today at 3 P.M., Symphony Hall, Newark. Tickets: $12-$44. The Winter Pops Series continues. Friday at 8 P.M., State Theater, New Brunswick; Saturday at 8:30 P.M., Count Basie Theater, Red Bank; Next Sunday at 3 P.M., Symphony Hall, Newark. Tickets: $18-$37. (800) ALLEGRO or (201) 624-8203.

RIDGEWOOD UNITED METHODIST CHURCH The New Jersey Choral Society presents "American Classics" featuring spirituals, as well as patriotic, barbershop and Broadway songs. Today at 4 P.M. Tickets: $14; $12 for students and the elderly. 100 Dayton Street, Van Neste Square, Ridgewood. (201) 652-3590.

STATE THEATER Mike Burstyn performs Broadway favorites, Israeli popular songs and the music of Yiddish theater. Next Sunday at 2 P.M. Tickets: $16-$25. 15 Livingston Avenue, New Brunswick. (908) 246-7469.

TEMPLE SHALOM, ABERDEEN Temple Shalom Choir presents love songs, wedding songs and music from Jerusalem. Tonight at 7 P.M. Tickets: $12; $8 for students and the elderly. 5 Ayrmont Lane, Aberdeen. (908) 566-2621.

TURNING POINT Traditional Folk with Woods Tea Co. Today at 7:30 P.M. Tickets: $10. Alison Brown. Thursday at 8 P.M. Tickets: $14. Savoy Brown. Friday at 8 and 10:30 P.M. Tickets: $15. An Evening of Acoustic Blues with Keb Mo. Saturday at 8 and 11 P.M. Tickets: $15. Megan McDonough and Debi Smith. Next Sunday at 7:30 P.M. Tickets: $15. 468 Piermont Avenue, Piermont, N.Y. (914) 359-1089.

UNION CONGREGATIONAL CHURCH, UPPER MONTCLAIR The Oratorio Society of New Jersey presents a concert featuring the works of Beethoven. Saturday at 8 P.M. Tickets: $12; $10 for students and the elderly. 176 Copper Avenue, Park and Valley Road, Upper Montclair. (201) 785-4125.

WILLIAM PATERSON COLLEGE The New Music Festival continues with a performance by the New Jersey Percussion Ensemble and the New Jersey New Music Ensemble. Monday at 8 P.M. Free. Shea Center for Performing Arts, 300 Pompton Road, Wayne. (201) 595-2371.

Theater

BICKFORD THEATER, MORRIS MUSEUM "Driving Miss Daisy." Thursdays, Fridays and Saturdays at 8 P.M.; Sundays at 2 P.M. Through April 4. Tickets: $17.50; $15 for students and the elderly. Morris Museum, 6 Normandy Heights Road, Morristown. (201) 538-8069.

BLACK RIVER PLAYHOUSE "Relatively Speaking," a comedy by Alan Ayckbourn. Friday and Saturday at 8 P.M. Tickets: $12. Grove Street, Chester. (908) 879-7304.

CENTENARY PERFORMING ARTS GUILD The Centenary Stage Company presents "Little Shop of Horrors." Sunday at 1:30 P.M.; Thursday at 7:30 P.M.; Friday and Saturday at 8 P.M. Tickets: $15-$17.50. 400 Jefferson Street, Hackettstown. (908) 979-0900.
