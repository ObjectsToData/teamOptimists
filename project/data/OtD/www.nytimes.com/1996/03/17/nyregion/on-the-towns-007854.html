ON THE TOWNS


Published: March 17, 1996

An opinionated guide to cultural and recreational goings-on around the state this week. To submit items for consideration, write to On the Towns, Sunday New Jersey Section, The New York Times, 229 West 43d Street, New York, N.Y. 10036, or send a fax to (212) 556-7219.

MUSIC

ATLANTIC COMMUNITY COLLEGE The Singer-Songwriter Showcase presents Cheryl Wheeler, storyteller, singer and folk songwriter. Friday at 8 P.M. Tickets: $7.50-$20. Walter Edge Theater, 5100 Black Horse Pike, Mays Landing. (609) 343-4907.

CENTRAL UNITARIAN CHURCH, PARAMUS The Adelphi Chamber Orchestra plays music of Copland and Weill and the premiere of "Adelphi Serenade" by Richard Lane, written for the Adelphi Chamber Orchestra. Today at 4 P.M. Free. 156 Forest Avenue, Paramus. (201) 664-5933.

CONGREGATION AGUDATH ISRAEL Karen Zereconsky, pianist, plays Beethoven, Brahms and Rachmaninoff. Today at 3 P.M. Tickets: $10-$12. 20 Academy Road, Caldwell. (201) 226-3600.

FAIRLEIGH DICKINSON UNIVERSITY The Orchestra of St. Peter by the Sea, featuring the Rev. Alphonse Stephenson, conductor. Saturday at 8 P.M. Tickets: $23. Recreation Center. "Traditional Music of China," featuring Yi Yang, zitherist, and Tao Chen, flutist. Next Sunday, 3 to 5 P.M. Tickets: $5; $2 for children and the elderly. Hartman Lounge, 285 Madison Avenue, Route 124, Madison. (201) 443-8661.

HEADQUARTERS PLAZA HOTEL New Philharmonic of New Jersey presents the Music for Singles series, featuring Basia Danilow, violinist, and Victor Keremedjiev, guitarist, who play classical and Gypsy folk music. Friday at 8 P.M. Tickets: $12-$15; optional pre-concert dinner, $15. 3 Headquarters Plaza, Speedwell Avenue, Morristown. (201) 267-0206.

JOHN HARMS CENTER FOR THE ARTS The Bergen Philharmonic Orchestra, featuring Ann Kim, violinist, and Ye Zhu, pianist. Saturday at 8:30 P.M. Tickets: $20; $17.50 for students and the elderly. 30 North Van Brunt Street, Englewood. (201) 567-3600.

MAXWELL'S The Big Geraniums and Deni Bonet. Friday at 10 P.M. Tickets: $7. Edsel, Kepone and Ditch Croaker. Saturday at 10 P.M. Tickets: $6. 1039 Washington Street, Hoboken. (201) 798-4064.

OLD LIBRARY THEATER John Gorka, singer. Thursday at 7:30 P.M. Tickets: $13-$15; $11-$13 for students and the elderly. 12-56 River Road, Fair Lawn. (201) 794-3558.

NEW JERSEY SYMPHONY ORCHESTRA "Beethoven and Beyond," featuring the Kalichstein-Laredo-Robinson Trio. Today at 3 P.M., John Harms Center for the Arts, Englewood. Tickets: $12-$36. The Masterworks Series continues with Nokuthula Ngwenyama, violist, and Christopher Collins Lee, violinist, performing Beethoven, Debussy and Grieg. Thursday at 8 P.M., John Harms Theater, Englewood. Friday at 8 P.M., State Theater, New Brunswick. Saturday at 8:30 P.M., Count Basie Theater, Red Bank. Next Sunday at 3 P.M., Symphony Hall, Newark. Tickets: $12-$44. (800) ALLEGRO or (201) 624-8203.

STOCKTON PERFORMING ARTS CENTER The Bridgeton Symphony Masterworks Series, featuring Pip Clarke, violinist, performs Beethoven, Brahms, Barber and Haydn. Today at 4 P.M.; pre-concert discussion led by Robert Schiavinato, conductor, at 3 P.M. Tickets: $15-$20. Jim Leeds Road, Pomona. (609) 652-9000.

TURNING POINT John Mayall and the Bluesbreakers. Tuesday at 7:30 and 10 P.M. Tickets: $25. Loudon Wainwright 3d. Wednesday at 7:30 and 10 P.M. Tickets: $17.50. Cheryl Wheeler. Thursday at 8 P.M. Tickets: $15. Robin and Linda Williams. Friday at 9 P.M. Tickets: $12.50. The Sonny Rhodes Blues Band. Saturday at 10 P.M. Tickets: $10. 468 Piermont Avenue, Piermont, N.Y. (914) 359-1089.

WATCHUNG ARTS CENTER The Fox Hills Bluegrass Band. Tonight at 7 P.M. Tickets: $8. Watchung Circle, Watchung. (908) 753-0190.

Theater

BLACK RIVER PLAYHOUSE "Relatively Speaking," a comedy by Alan Ayckbourn. Fridays and Saturdays at 8 P.M. Through March 30. Tickets: $12. Grove Street, Chester. (908) 879-7304.

CENTENARY PERFORMING ARTS GUILD The Centenary Stage Company presents "Little Shop of Horrors." Sundays at 1:30 P.M.; Thursdays at 7:30 P.M.; Fridays and Saturdays at 8 P.M. Through March 30. Tickets: $15-$17.50. 400 Jefferson Street, Hackettstown. (908) 979-0900.

CIRCLE PLAYHOUSE The Circle Players present "Arsenic and Old Lace," by Joseph Kesselring. Today at 2 P.M. Tickets: $9-$11; $10 for students and the elderly. 416 Victoria Avenue, Piscataway. (98) 968-7555.

CIVIL WAR DRILL HALL The Players' Guild of Leonia presents "Dancing at Lughnasa," by Brian Friel. Today at 3 P.M. Through next Sunday. Tickets: $12; $11 for students and the elderly. 130 Grand Avenue, Leonia. (201) 947-9606 or (201) 461-5976.

COUNTY COLLEGE OF MORRIS "The Wizard of Oz," a musical. Thursday, Friday and Saturday at 8 P.M. Tickets: $10; $7 for children. Student Center, County College of Morris, 214 Center Grove Road, Randolph. (201) 328-5225.

CROSSROADS THEATER COMPANY "Fear Itself," a new play by Eugene Lee. Tuesdays, Wednesdays and Fridays at 8 P.M.; Thursdays at 11 and 8 P.M.; Saturdays at 3 and 8 P.M.; Sundays at 3 and 7:30 P.M. Through April 7. Tickets: $22-$32. 7 Livingston Avenue, New Brunswick. (908) 249-5560.
