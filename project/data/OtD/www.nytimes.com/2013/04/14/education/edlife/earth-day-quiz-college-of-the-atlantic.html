Human Ecology: Our Favorite Planet

The College of the Atlantic, in Bar Harbor, Me., pioneered human ecology, a discipline that blends natural sciences, social sciences, the arts and humanities to find solutions to environmental challenges. Faculty members broke tradition — no multiple-choice exams at this college — to write quiz questions in celebration of Earth Day, April 22.
