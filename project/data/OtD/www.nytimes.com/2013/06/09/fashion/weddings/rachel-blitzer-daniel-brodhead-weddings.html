Rachel Blitzer, Daniel Brodhead

Published: June 9, 2013

Rachel Renee Blitzer, a daughter of Patricia Molina and Robert D. Blitzer of Brewster, N.Y., was married in New Haven Saturday to Daniel Degnan Brodhead, the son of Cynthia D. Brodhead and Richard H. Brodhead of Durham, N.C. Christopher J. Randall, a Connecticut justice of the peace, officiated at Yale University Berkeley College, in a ceremony led by Robert F. Doyle, a friend of the couple.

Enlarge This Image
Mattion Grazier

Though the bride and groom were undergraduates at Yale together for three years, they did not meet until years after graduation, at the Yale-Harvard football game in 2007.

Mrs. Brodhead, 32, is an associate at the New York law firm Skadden, Arps, Slate, Meagher & Flom. She received a law degree from the University of Chicago.

Her father is an associate professor of pharmacology and systems therapeutics and a professor of psychiatry at the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai in New York, where he oversees a laboratory investigating molecular aspects of memory formation. Her mother is a fiduciary accountant at the Meyer Handelman Company, an investment advisory firm in Rye Brook, N.Y.

Mr. Brodhead, 33, is a producer of digital advertising at R/GA, a New York advertising agency.

His mother is the chairwoman of the North Carolina Humanities Council, and until 2004 was a counsel at Wiggin & Dana, a law firm in New Haven. His father is the president of Duke University and was the dean of Yaleâ€™s undergraduate college from 1993 to 2003.
