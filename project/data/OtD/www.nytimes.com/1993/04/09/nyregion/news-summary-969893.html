France's new Government announced a major campaign to fight urban crime and illegal immigration, but it is under fire from human rights groups after three shootings in which the police killed two young men and wounded a third. A2 YELTSIN TRIES TO BUY VOTES

President Clinton sent Congress a $1.52 trillion budget full of details showing how he wants to reduce the Government's deficit while spending more money on public works, education and retraining jobless workers. He promised to fight for his proposals even though the envisioned spending exceeded Congressional limits. A1 FOR A CHANGE, A ROAD MAP

An outbreak of a flu-like intestinal illness has stricken hundreds of people in the Milwaukee area, and the city has been seized with alarm by the suspicion that the cause may be contamination of the municipal water supply. A1 FINAL ARGUMENTS IN BEATING CASE
